Measuring Straight lines with ruler

The steps for measuring are:-

1. Place the ruler along the length of the object such that the 0 mark coincides with one edge of it. The reading will tell us the length of the object.

2. If the 0 mark of a ruler is broken,the next clear mark should coincide with the one edge of the object.Then we need to subtract the reading of the other end from the starting point.
